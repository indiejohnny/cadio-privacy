Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Cadio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the Cadio app.

Data we collect

Cadio does not require an account and does not collect or store any personal information on our servers. All app data (saved programs, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

Advertising

The free version of Cadio displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings. For more information visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-app purchases

Cadio Pro is a one-time purchase processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Purchase and payment information is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We use RevenueCat to manage purchase verification. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. For more information visit: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Third party services

Cadio uses the following third party services: Google AdMob (advertising), RevenueCat (purchase management). These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data collection.

Data storage

All user data including saved interval programs and app settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children

Cadio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact us at: cadioapp@gmail.com
